Bowling Teams

A calculator that can be fed bowling averages. It will then output equal teams for the purpose of fairly dividing players. This came from my attendance at a bowling club, where we'd spend a while each night figuring out fair teams as averages would change each week, as would those in attendance. It was a great hit among my friends at the bowling group and saved us lots of time each week. Uses Javascript*, Bootstrap, HTML/CSS.
